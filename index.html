Introduction to Computer and Social Networks

What is a computer network?

A computer network is a system that connects multiple devices mainly for communication and sharing resources. These networks can be through advanced wireless technology such as Bluetooth and Wi-Fi or wired connections through physical cables. They range from small Wi-Fi setups to large scale networks such as the internet.

What is its purpose?

  • Easy Communication
  • Resource Sharing
  • Centralised Data Management
  • Remote Access
  • Security

What is a social network and how does it work?

A social network is a platform or a website that allows users and organisations to communicate, collaborate and share information with each other. In most of these platforms users can create profiles, friend, follow and message other people. Some examples of social networks are Facebook, Instagram and Snapchat.

What is its purpose?

  • Entertainment
  • Marketing
  • Building Relationships
  • Sharing Information
  • Education
